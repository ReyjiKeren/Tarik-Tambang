HOW TO DOMINATE

01 // THE GOAL

Pull the Gravity Core to your side of the screen. Keep the tension high and outsmart your opponent's gravity manipulations.

02 // TAP (MICRO-PULL)

Tap quickly to maintain tension and perform small, annoying pulls that destabilize your opponent.

03 // CHARGE & RELEASE

HOLD directly on the screen to charge your Anti-Gravity Boots.
RELEASE to unleash a massive shockwave pull!

Warning: You are vulnerable while charging!

04 // MULTIPLAYER

1. "Create Room" to generate a unique 4-Digit Freq ID.
2. Share the ID with your opponent.
3. Opponent chooses "Join Room" and enters the ID.
4. The system will auto-sync your realities.
